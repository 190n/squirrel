Shoot your opponent to knock them out of the safe area and score a point. Fuel and ammo are limited. Fuel refills when you are on the ground. Ammo can be reloaded manually, but will reload automatically when it runs out.
Player 1 Player 2

A: fly left J: fly left
S: shoot down K: shoot down
D: fly right L: fly right
R: reload U: reload
F: shoot H: shoot
ESC: pause
LT: throttle
LS: fly (left/right)
RS: aim (any direction)
RB: shoot
X (on Xbox-style controller): reload
START: pause
